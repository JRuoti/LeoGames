VIDEOGAMES/AVARICE

Concept

First-person action adventure with puzzle elements made with Unreal Engine 5.
The main focus of the gameplay is the movement system, which is enhanced by the interaction with magical structures that allow players to modify the environment and express their skill.
I initially proposed to make a movement-centric game, mainly because I was playing Marvel's Spider-Man and I was excited by the idea of designing a fun motion system

Contributions

I worked to identify the mechanics we needed to create our movement system and how the magical structures were supposed to work.
Me and the other programmer were responsible for determining the feasibility of our gameplay in realization terms.
I wrote game design documents on Confluence.

I was the lead level designer for the project, so I was writing documentation and reviewing the levels of my colleagues.
In addition, I got my hands on the engine by making levels from scratch and dressing the scenes.
I was mainly responsible for the character controller, context provider and save system blueprints.
